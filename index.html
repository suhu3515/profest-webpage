ABOUT KARMA


Its the annual national level techno managerial cultural extravaganza of the KMCT College of Engineering. Karma invites innovative minds to compete with each other, share ideas and educate people about new technological advancements and be a part of the tech platform.

Spectacular talent stretching boundaries to inspire the participating students and the rapt audience alike. A gigantic range of events from advanced robotics challenges to entrepreneurship hunts promising to zap up the cranial indices of participants.Skill, genius and knowledge trading infused with handful of fun, and a titanic prize money sum of above half a million rupees to make Karma one of the most unique techÂ­carnivals of the country.

Paper Presentation

Win Rs: 8,000/-
First Price : 5,000/-
Second Price : 3,000/-
Rules and Regulations

  1. Students will need to register on our portal or register on spot to participate in the contest.
  2. Team Event : 2 or 3 participants per team. ( 1 Team leader + Team member).
  3. More than one team can participate from the same college.
  4. For team formation, both members would need to be from the same college/institute.
  5. Participants should not present more than one paper.
  6. Participants should send their Abstract to email karmaprofest@gmail.com on or before 18/03/2020.
  7. Abstract should contain Name, Year, Department, Institute, course name, contact Number, Topic. Abstract should not exceed word limit of 400 Words.
  8. Selection of abstract will be through review process. The contestants will be informed those who are selected.
  9. Teams must submit a softcopy & a hardcopy of the final Paper preferrable IEEE format to the panel on the date of presentation
    (http://ieeeauthorcenter.ieee.org/wp_content/uploads/JQE-Template.doc)
  10. Papers must be written and presented in English.
  11. Duration of paper presentation should not exceed 20 minutes and 10 minutes for interactive section.
  12. Participants can use in ppt/latex
  13. The decision of the coordinators and judgement panel will be final.
